Across education, media, operations, faith and community, I have built over 12 brands and counting. These listed here are the projects I've founded, co-founded, or currently lead.

Infrastructure & Products

EdTech · B2B

HammetLabs / Hammet

Co-founder & CPO — 2025–Present

HammetLabs builds institutional infrastructure for Africa's emerging economy, starting with education. Hammet, its flagship product, is a B2B AI Studies curriculum licensed directly to secondary schools across Nigeria, equipping JSS1–SS3 students with structured AI literacy and verifiable credentials through the Hammet Digital Passport system.

Community · Education

Konoha University

Co-founder & COO — 2023–Present

A digital campus and learning community with thousands of participants across cohorts, retreats, virtual events and more. Konoha runs structured seasonal learning programs, leadership pathways, and community infrastructure for the next generation of African builders and thinkers.

Community · Financial Education

Konoha Financial Community

Operations Manager — 2024–Present

A community of financially savvy individuals. Our aim is to educate youths on financial literacy, thereby improving the nation's economy overall. We held the first ever student-led conference in UNILAG in 2024, and as at 2025, we have hosted 6 financial conferences across 4 federal universities and multiple virtual financial workshops.
