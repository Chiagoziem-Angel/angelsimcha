HammetLabs / Hammet

Co-founder & CPO — 2025–Present

HammetLabs builds institutional infrastructure for Africa's emerging economy, starting with education. Hammet — its flagship product — is a B2B AI Studies curriculum licensed directly to private secondary schools across Nigeria, equipping JSS1–SS3 students with structured AI literacy and verifiable credentials through a Digital Passport system.
